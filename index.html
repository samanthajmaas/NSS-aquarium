Locations

Great Barrier Reef
Great Barrier Reef Information
One of Australia’s most remarkable natural gifts, the Great Barrier Reef is blessed with the breathtaking beauty of the world’s largest coral reef. The reef contains an abundance of marine life and comprises of over 3000 individual reef systems and coral cays and literally hundreds of picturesque tropical islands with some of the worlds most beautiful sun-soaked, golden beaches.
Cambodia
Diving in Cambodia
Cambodia is a beautiful place that is gifted with rich scenery and very eye-catching architecture. From temples, fields, beaches, jungles and beautiful waterfalls, one can find it all in Cambodia. Apart from all these great things that make Cambodia what it is, scuba diving is also very popular there. With its clear blue waters, colorful marine life and secluded islands, scuba diving in Cambodia is the best option. If you have that zeal inside and want to try something adventurous, make sure to not miss out the chance to experience this.
South America
Diving in South America
If sharks, rays, whales and dolphins are your thing then go to the Revillagigedo islands of Mexico. Like The Galapagos, the islands are at the meeting of cool and warm currents. This creates upwellings of nutrient-rich water which supports the excess of marine life.
Argentina
Argentina Diving
Peninsula Valdes, Ushuaia, and Puerto Madryn are the main scuba diving destinations. Peninsula Valdes is a thriving UNESCO World Heritage Site and a mecca for nature lovers. During June to December, southern right whales descend upon this area and visitors can watch from shore or admire them underwater.
